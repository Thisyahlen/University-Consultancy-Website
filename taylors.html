DEPARTMENT OF ENGINEERING

WHY ENGINEERING AT TAYLOR'S

1st Engineering School in Malaysia to be accepted as a collaborator in the prestigious CDIO™ Initiative.

1st Engineering School outside the US to offer this Scholars Program recognised by the National Academy of Engineering.

Taylor’s Racing Team (TRT) is an avenue for students to unleash their potential by challenging themselves and putting their knowledge into practice.

Strategic alliances with top industry players such as Shell, Petronas, Honeywell and SCOMI Group Bhd.

    Specialising in:

  1. Mechanical Engineering
  2. Electronic and Electrical Engineering
  3. Chemical Engineering

    Entry Requirements:

  1. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent with 5 credits for entry with Pre-University/Foundation qualification
  2. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent with 3 credits for entry with Diploma qualification
  3. Pass with min. CGPA 2.00
  4. Students with other qualifications will be considered on a case-by-case basis

    Course Fees:

  1. Mechanical Engineering - RM 139,500
  2. Electronic and Electrical Engineering - RM 139,500
  4. Chemical Engineering - RM 139,500

BACHELOR OF LAWS (LL.B)

WHY LAW AT TAYLOR'S

Students gain insights into the legal profession and hands-on training before they even graduate.

We groom future lawyers with a global perspective and understanding of international issues.

We have established close collaboration with some of the most prestigious law firms and members of the legal profession in the country.

Students can opt to articulate to Taylor's UK partner universities.

    Overseas Articulation Options:

  1. CARDIFF UNIVERSITY
  2. UNIVERSITY OF LEEDS
  3. UNIVERSITY OF LIVERPOOL
  4. UNIVERSITY OF READING

    Entry Requirements:

  1. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent with 5 credits (including English) for entry with pre-university / foundation qualification (Except for UEC)
  2. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent with 3 credits (including English) for entry with Diploma qualification.
  3. Pass STPM with CGPA 2.67 in 2 subjects with at least a C in Pengajian Am

    Course Fees:

  1. RM 104,520

DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTING

WHY COMPUTING AT TAYLOR'S

Equal certification from Taylor’s University and the University of the West of England (UWE), UK, with the option to transfer to UWE in the final year.

The School encourages and enables students to undertake innovative projects in its quest to nurture budding technopreneurs.

Strategic alliances with industry partners facilitate dynamic knowledge exchange, enabling our students to reap the rewards of these collaborations.

The Premier Digital Tech Institutions provide an optimum ecosystem to prepare graduates to meet industry expectations.

    Entry Requirements:

  1. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent with 5 credits (including Additional Mathematics) for entry with Pre-University / Foundation qualification
  2. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent with 3 credits (including Additional Mathematics) for entry with Diploma / Pre-University qualification
  3. Pass with min. CGPA 2.50

    Course Fees:

  1. Bachelor of Computer Science(Hons) - RM 97,500

SCHOOL OF MEDIA AND COMMUNICATION

WHY MEDIA AND COMMUNICATIONS AT TAYLOR'S

Equal certification from Taylor’s University and the University of the West of England (UWE), UK, with the option to transfer to UWE in the final year.

Our students are placed with some of the most notable local and international establishments in the industry during the internship programme.

ProPassion Communications is a Public Relations (PR) consultancy managed by the final year degree students which collaborates with local and international clients.

Students work on actual projects with clients such as DiGi, Dutch Lady, EcoWorld, Escape Room and more.

    Entry Requirements:

  1. SPM / O-Level or equivalent, 5 credits including English for entry with pre-university / foundation qualification.
  2. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent, 3 credits including English for entry with Diploma qualification.
  3. Pass with min. CGPA 2.00

    Course Fees:

  1. Bachelor of Mass Communication(Hons) - RM 95,500

TAYLOR'S BUSINESS SCHOOL

WHY BUSINESS AT TAYLOR'S

Taylor's Business School offers dual award degree programmes in collaboration with University of the West of England (UWE) & Queensland University of Technology (QUT)

Our programmes are recognised and qualifies for exemptions from professional bodies.

Students are exposed to real-life scenario and industry-relevant experiences that enhance student engagement.

We nurture and develop students’ entrepreneurial capabilities, and instilling the right foundation in sustaining their business growth.

    Entry Requirements:

  1. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent with 5 credits (including a Pass in Mathematics and English) for entry with pre-university/ foundation qualification (Except for UEC)
  2. Pass SPM / O-Level or equivalent with 3 credits (including a Pass in Mathematics and English) for entry with Diploma qualification
  3. Pass with min. CGPA 2.00

    Course Fees:

  1. Bachelor of Business (Hons) - RM 102,500
