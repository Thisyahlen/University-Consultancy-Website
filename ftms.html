ACCOUNTING & BUSINESS MANAGEMENT

Accounting & Business Management offers quality programmes which equip students with the skills and knowledge to seek employment in a wide range of business areas such as Accounting, Finance, Business, Marketing and Management. In today’s competitive landscape, and the ever-changing economy, learning is inseparable from work. It is the process by which knowledge is generated, transferred and put in to action.

The school develops students’ ability to take responsibility for skills development through research and teamwork. Students are expected to understand the practical and theoretical issues facing the global business environment and develop ethical and innovative solutions.

The school’s extensive global network enables students to interact in a diverse, demanding and lively environment.

Accounting & Business Management looks forward to welcoming you to an excellent and rewarding experience in your life and future career.

    Specialising in:

  1. BA(Hons) in Business Management
  2. BA(Hons) in Human Resource Management
  3. BSc(Hons) in Accounting and Finance
  4. Diploma in Finance
  5. Diploma in Accounting
  6. Diploma in Business

    Entry Requirements:

  1. Two (2) Principles in STPM / 'A' Level
  2. Minimum 3 Credits in SPM / 'O' Level
  3. Diploma / 'A' Level / Foundation / Matriculation or any equivalent qualification recognised by the Malaysian government will be accepted

    Course Fees:

  1. BA(Hons) in Business Management - RM 39,000
  2. BA(Hons) in Human Resource Management - RM 39,000
  3. BSc(Hons) in Accounting and Finance - RM 39,000
  4. Diploma in Finance - RM 15,000
  5. Diploma in Accounting - RM 15,000
  6. Diploma in Business - RM 15,000
